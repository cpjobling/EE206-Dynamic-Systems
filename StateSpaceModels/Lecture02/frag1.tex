The Laplace transform of a vector $\mathbf{v}(t)$ is a vector
$\mathbf{V}(s)$. The elements of $\mathbf{V}(s)$ are the Laplace
transforms of the corresponding elements of the vector
$\mathbf{v}(t)$.
For array
\begin{displaymath}
  \mathbf{v}(t) = \left[
  \begin{array}{c}
    v_1(t) \\
    v_2(t) \\
    \vdots \\
    v_n(t) \\
  \end{array}
  \right]
\end{displaymath}
